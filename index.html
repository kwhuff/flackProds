Flack Productions Creative Video


Flack Productions is a small, one-man videography company with big ideas and a great imagination. Michael delivers your story with creativity and a uniqueness made possible only by your vision and personal experience.

Being nonintrusive and discreet allows Michael to capture those most special and genuine moments when people aren’t thinking about the cameras being on them. Weddings, in particular, are a beautiful and fleeting chance to capture raw and powerful emotions, sincere relationships, and genuine personalities.

Flack Productions wants to collaborate closely with you to tell your story in a most meaningful and impactful way. Be it a wedding or love story, corporate event, commercial, or a promo for your very own business…your story matters; and Flack Productions can help.
